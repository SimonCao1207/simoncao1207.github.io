Hi! I’m Nam Cao, a Master’s student at KAIST’s Kim Jaechul Graduate School of AI (KAIST AI), advised by Prof. Se-Young Yun in OSI Lab. I also earned my B.S. in Computer Science at KAIST.

My research is on efficient and safe large language models — making inference faster and cheaper without giving up quality. These days I focus on speculative decoding, diffusion language models, and the adversarial robustness of vision-language models.

Feel free to email me if you’d like to chat about efficient LLMs.
